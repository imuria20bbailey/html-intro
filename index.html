Empowering Futures Through Sports And Innovation.

Uganda is one of the Countries in the world with alot of Homeless and unforcused youth.
Employment opportunities are limited due to greed by the elderly and inadequate skills among the youth.
The highest Population of the Ugandas economy are the youth. As an academy,
we want to give an opportunuty to this youth and children to discover thier abilities and talents so that they can create jobs for themselves.

PURPOSE

To make a meaningfull difference in the live of vulnerablechildren and youth, enabling them to reach thier fullest potential


OUR VISION

A world where every child regardless of their circumstances has the opportunity to grow up in a safe, naturing enviroment. Through our dynamic sports and innovative academy, we strive to inspire and equip young minds with the skills, confidence and resillence needed to overcome challenges and thrive in life.


OUR MISSION

To be the transformers of lives of homeless infants, children and youth by establishing a nurturing home envoroment where they can flourish by the creation of a dynamic sports and innovative academy which will inspire these young individuals to pursue thier passions and dreams, fostering a profound sense of purpose and belonging .
